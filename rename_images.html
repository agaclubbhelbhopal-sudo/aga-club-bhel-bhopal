Image Renamer Tool

How to Use This Tool:

1. Enter a prefix for your images (e.g., "diwali", "holi", "event")
2. Select multiple JPG files you want to rename
3. Preview the renamed files
4. Download the renamed files

Rename Your Images

Preview

Select files and click "Preview Renamed Files" to see the changes.

Note: This tool renames your files locally in your browser. The renamed files will be downloaded to your computer. You can then move them to the appropriate folder in your website's images directory.

Example: If you enter "diwali" as prefix and select 3 images, they will be renamed as "diwali1.jpg", "diwali2.jpg", and "diwali3.jpg"
