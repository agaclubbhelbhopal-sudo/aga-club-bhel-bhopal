Benefits of Membership

  • Participation in all club events and activities
  • Voting rights in club decisions
  • Opportunity to volunteer and contribute to community service
  • Access to exclusive member-only events
  • Networking opportunities with fellow community members
  • Discounts on select club merchandise and services

Membership Requirements

To become a member of AGA Club Bhel Bhopal, you must:

  • Be a resident of BHEL Township or have a connection to the community
  • Agree to abide by the club's constitution and bylaws
  • Pay the annual membership fee as determined by the committee
  • Participate in at least one club activity or event per year
